Free Summer Internship

Program Details

As we strive to enhance the professional development opportunities for the students of AKTU, Softpro India is offering Free Summer Internship Program in collaboration with Dr A P J Abdul Kalam Technical University under the MoU services signed with Softpro India & AKTU, specifically tailored for the Ist, IInd & IIIrd year students pursuing B.Tech in Computer Science, Information Technology and CS Allied Branches, Mechanical, Civil, Electrical, Electronics. This program aims to provide students with valuable industry exposure, hands-on experience in the latest technologies, and an avenue for practical application of theoretical knowledge.

Importance of Summer Internship

1. Bridging the Gap between Academia and Industry : Summer Internships provide students with an invaluable opportunity to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge acquired in classrooms and practical application in real-world scenarios. By working on live projects and interacting with industry professionals, students gain insights into industry practices, trends, and challenges, thus preparing them for the transition from academia to the professional realm.

2. Skill Enhancement and Professional Development: Engaging in summer internship programs allows students to enhance their technical skills, communication abilities, teamwork, and problem solving capabilities. Exposure to cutting-edge technologies and hands-on experience in project execution equips students with the competencies required to thrive in today's dynamic and competitive job market.

3. Networking and Career Opportunities: Summer internships offer students a platform to network with professionals, build meaningful connections, and explore potential career opportunities. Building a strong professional network early in their academic journey can significantly enhance students' prospects for securing employment post-graduation.

Internship Session
a.) Online / Offline Sessions:

The internship will commence with intensive training sessions covering a wide array of topics including but not limited to: i. Programming languages such as Java, Python, PHP, C# etc. ii. Web development frameworks like Django, Spring, Laravel, AngularJS, React, etc. iii. Database management systems and data analytics tools. iv. Emerging technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Internet of Things, etc. v. Technologies like IoT, PLC SCADA, Embedded System, AutoCAD, Solidwork & STAAD.PRO

b.) Project Work:

Following the training period, interns will be assigned to real-world projects under the guidance and mentorship of experienced professionals from Softpro India. This hands-on experience will enable students to apply theoretical concepts in practical scenarios, thereby honing their problem solving skills and enhancing their technical proficiency.

c.) Evaluation and Certification:

At the conclusion of the internship, students will undergo an assessment to evaluate their performance and proficiency gained during the program. Upon successful completion, participants will receive certificates acknowledging their accomplishments during the internship.

d.)Placement Opportunity:

Students who will do Internship from Softpro will also be offered Placement Opportunity in their final year.
